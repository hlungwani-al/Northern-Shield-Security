About

The shield is the symbol of our protection and that all who carry it are our protectors. It is a symbol of love and courage to do what it takes to keep our community safe!

  • March 2019

    NORTHERN SHIELD SECURITY AND TRAINING WAS BORN

    Northern Shield Security and Training was established with a strong commitment to enhancing safety standards in south african regions.
    From its inception, the company aimed to provide top-quality security solutions and rigorous training programs. Emphasizing community trust and professionalism, it quickly gained recognition for its innovative approach and reliability in the security industry.

  • December 2020

    OUR FIRST OFFICE

    Northern Shield Security made a bold move in December 2020 by opening an office in the stunning village of Ha-Mutoti, near the Royal Garden. In the years since, we've gone from strength to strength, growing into the best security company around with a reputation for excellent and reliable security services. We're committed to providing you with peace of mind and safety. Join us on our journey as we continue to excel and expand our services.

  • December 2022

    OUR SECOND OFFICE

    Since opening our second office in December 2022 at Khubvi village, Northern Shield Security has expanded our reach and presence in the region. The new site provides convenient and reliable access to our services for clients in and around Khubvi, strengthening our commitment to delivering unparalleled security solutions. Our strategic location allows us to swiftly and efficiently respond to security needs in the area, ensuring you're always protected.
